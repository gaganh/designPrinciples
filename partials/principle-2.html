A high percentage of effects in any large system is caused by a low percentage of variables.

  • Proposed by economist Vilfredo Pareto, who observed that 20% of the Italian people possessed 80% of the wealth.
  • Applying the 80/20 Rule means identifying and focusing resources on the critical 20%, beyond which yields diminishing returns.
  • Used to assess the relative value of elements, target areas of redesign and optimization.
